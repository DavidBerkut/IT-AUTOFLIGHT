If you need to customize umin and umax for the AP to fly correctly, and want to keep drop in upgrades, add this to the -set or -main files.
Must be below or outside <sim> </sim>
If you do not know how to tune umin and umax, I don't recommend adding this.
<it-autoflight>
	<settings>
		<no-generics>1</no-generics>
		<roll>
			<umin></umin>
			<umax></umax>
		</roll>
		<pitch>
			<umin></umin>
			<umax></umax>
		</pitch>
		<flch>
			<umin></umin>
			<umax></umax>
		</flch>
	</settings>
</it-autoflight>
